About me

Dr. Liu Yong is a Data Scientist, Manager with Link Analytics Center, NTUC Link since November 2017. Prior to that, he was a research scientist in Data Analytics Department, Institute for Infocomm Research (I2R), A*STAR from November 2015 to October 2017. He received Ph.D. from the School of Computer Science and Engineering at Nanyang Technological University in June 2016 and B.S. from the Department of Electronic Science and Technology at University of Science and Technology of China in 2008.

Research Interests

My current research interests include:

  • Data Mining: recommender systems, computational advertising, social media mining, network embedding

  • Machine Learning: matrix computation, learning to rank, online learning, deep learning, Bayesian learning

  • Bioinformatics: drug-target interaction prediction, synthetic lethality prediction
