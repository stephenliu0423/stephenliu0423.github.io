About me

I am a research scientist in Data Analytics Department, Institute for Infocomm Research (I2R), A*STAR, Singapore. I received Ph.D. from the School of Computer Science and Engineering at Nanyang Technological University in 2016 and B.S. from the Department of Electronic Science and Technology at University of Science and Technology of China in 2008.

Research Interests

My current research interests include:

  • Data Mining: Recommender Systems; Computational Advertising; Social Media Mining; Network Embedding

  • Machine Learning: Matrix Computation; Learning to Rank; Online Learning; Deep Learning; Bayesian Learning

  • Bioinformatics: Drug-target Interaction Prediction; Synthetic Lethality Prediction
